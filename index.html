Jenevieve Chang

“Great stories happen to those who can tell them.”

Ira Glass

Hi, I’m Jenevieve. Welcome to my world. I write stories, perform words and develop scripts. I make things happen for audiences. I’m the author of The Good Girl of Chinatown.

A story is a journey, ultimately, for the audience – which is best structured according to the simple requirements of a beginning, middle and end.

In the beginning

Jenevieve’s formative years were dominated by dance, excelling in choreography. A dance and theatre career in Europe followed, where Jenevieve worked with companies such as the National Theatre, the Young Vic and Yellow Earth before undertaking her MA at the Central School of Speech and Drama. In 2008, Jenevieve was Artist-in-Residence in Beijing’s 798 Art District. As the Global Financial Crisis hit the rest of the world, Jenevieve decided to stay in China, the land of her grandparents’ stories. Moving to Shanghai, she continued to create shows, direct plays and appear in films.

Work

Revealing a story’s potential is at the heart of the development process. I develop scripts for screen and stage and have recently worked with Felix Media, Arclight Films and am currently a Development Executive at Screen Australia.
